Ingredients

  • 1 U+00bd tablespoons butter
  • 2 U+00bd cups whole kernel sweet corn
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Step 1: Heat a skillet over high heat. Heat butter in hot skillet until almost completely melted; stir in corn. Season corn with Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Cook seasoned corn until light golden brown, about 5 minutes.
